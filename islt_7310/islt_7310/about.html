About Me

Hello, my name is Jennifer Sharp. I am a grad student in the MLIS program, and I expect to graduate in December. I live in Anywhere County, USA with my husband and our cat. In our spare time we enjoy traveling and hiking together. I also love doing anything crafty or DIY-related. Sewing, woodworking, scrapbooking, crocheting, home improvement – basically anything that allows me to be creative and make things. Lately I have been working on learning to enjoy cooking and preparing healthy food.
For the past three years I have worked in the Youth Department at my local public library, and I love my job. It’s so rewarding getting to do something that I’m passionate about at work every day. I have little to no web application experience. After this course I hope to be able to create basic websites, but I’m not sure exactly how I will use them yet.
